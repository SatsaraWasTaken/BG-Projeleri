Chemistry


What is Chemistry?

Chemistry is the branch of science that deals with the properties, composition, and structure of elements and compounds, how they can change, and the energy that is released or absorbed when they change.


Some Remarkable Chemists


1- Maria Curie

Marie Salomea Skłodowska–Curie was a Polish and naturalized-French physicist and chemist who conducted pioneering research on radioactivity.

Born: November 7, 1867, Warsaw, Poland

Died: July 4, 1934, Passy, France

2- Dmitri Mendeleev

Dmitri Ivanovich Mendeleev was a Russian chemist and inventor. He is best known for formulating the Periodic Law and creating a version of the periodic table of elements.

Born: February 8, 1834, Tobolsk, Russia

Died: February 2, 1907, Saint Petersburg, Russia

3- Louis Pasteur

Louis Pasteur ForMemRS was a French chemist and microbiologist renowned for his discoveries of the principles of vaccination, microbial fermentation, and pasteurization.

Born: December 27, 1822, Dole, France

Died: September 28, 1895, Marnes-la-Coquette, France
